Welcome to PartyUP

PartyUP is a nightlife application that allows you to share short, ephemeral videos demonstrating the atmosphere at the venue you are visiting. If you want to plan your next party stop, you can view the videos submitted by other party animals at venues in your area to make an informed choice. Videos submitted to PartyUP are between 5 and 10 seconds long and may include a note from the submitter providing details about the atmosphere at the venue. A video is linked to the venue from which it was submitted, usually a bar or nightlife spot.

If you aren't out partying just yet, you can share a video on the pregame feed of the city you are in. This allows you to share your plans with other party people in your area. You also have the option of looking at the videos from other cities if you think those cities offer fun you are willing to travel for.

Venue Browsing

The venue list is the hub of of the PartyUP experience. From here you will initiate the two primary activities PartyUP has been created for: 1) submitting videos and 2) viewing the videos submitted by others.

1. Party Place Picker
Select a sticky city whose venues will be displayed in the venue list. Current Location uses your location to determine the city you are in. The available sticky cities are currently determined by the PartyUP development team.
2. About
Peruse documentation and acknowledgements.
3. Submit Video
Initiate the process of submitting a video.
4. Venue Filter
Filter the list of venues down to those containing the string you enter here. The All Parties Videos feed will be filtered down to those videos recorded at the venues allowed by the filter. The Pregame Videos feed is unaffected by the filter.
5. Local Videos
6. Venue Listing
Select a venue to see videos recorded there.
7. Loading Status

Recording

1. Cancel
Return to the venue list without submitting a video. Available even while the determining location box is present.
2. Camera Toggle
Toggle between the rear and front cameras.
3. Viewfinder
See what your camera is seeing.
4. Record
Hold to record a video. Disabled while your location is being determined. The progress ring changes color from pink to yellow when the minimum video length has been recorded.
